Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Florque ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our workflow orchestration and automation platform, including our Desktop, Mobile, and Web applications (collectively, the "Services").

This policy applies to both individual users (B2C) and business customers (B2B), including enterprise organizations and their employees who use our Services.

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, company name, job title, and profile information
  • Billing Information: Payment card details, billing address, and transaction history (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Workflow Data: Content you create, upload, or process through our Services, including task descriptions, automation rules, integration configurations, and related metadata
  • Communications: Messages, feedback, support requests, and other communications with us
  • Organization Data (B2B): Information about your organization, team members, user roles, and permissions

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use our Desktop, Mobile, or Web applications, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Features used, workflows created, execution logs, interaction patterns, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: Access times, pages viewed, error reports, and system activity
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Session cookies, preference cookies, and analytics cookies to enhance your experience
  • Location Information: General location based on IP address (we do not collect precise geolocation without your consent)

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

  • Integration Data: Information from third-party services you connect to Florque (e.g., email providers, project management tools, communication platforms)
  • Social Media: Profile information if you choose to sign in through social media accounts
  • Business Partners: Information from our resellers, partners, or referral sources

3.2 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications, updates, and security alerts

3.3 Business Operations

  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other policies
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Conduct analytics and research to improve our Services

3.4 AI and Automation

  • Train and improve our AI models to enhance workflow automation
  • Generate workflow suggestions and recommendations
  • Provide intelligent task orchestration and optimization

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL; sensitive data at rest is encrypted
  • Access Controls: Role-based access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Staff training on data protection and security practices
  • Incident Response: Procedures for detecting and responding to security incidents

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our Services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Maintain business records and analytics

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
