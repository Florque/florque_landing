So what actually is Florque?

Florque is an AI-powered project management autopilot designed to help individuals and small teams turn ideas into structured, executable work.

Traditional tools assume projects are already defined. Florque solves the harder problem: turning messy ideas into structured projects and helping execute them.

It transforms unstructured thoughts into clear project plans, organizes tasks into a connected work graph, and assists in executing work efficiently with AI. Florque aims to become an AI-native execution system where planning, prioritization, and project operations are partially automated, enabling solo builders, operators, and small teams to focus on building rather than managing the process.
