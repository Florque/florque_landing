So what actually is Florque?

Florque is a workflow management tool that acts like a copilot for your workflow, streamlining and organizing tasks for personal or professional projects. Picture planning your daughter’s birthday party: you’re thinking about booking a venue, sending invites, ordering a cake, and planning games. It’s a familiar process, much like preparing for a corporate event—say, launching a product or onboarding an employee—where you’re managing a series of steps, deadlines, and people, hoping nothing falls through the cracks.

Florque takes this to the next level by not only keeping your checklist on track but also actively searching for tools to execute tasks for you. For instance, if you need to send invites, Florque might suggest an email or Whatsapp MCP or even a platform's tool like Eventbrite and, with your approval, handle the task automatically. It builds a clear, step-by-step pipeline, flags any missing steps (like “Did you pick a theme for the party?” or “Should we schedule a team briefing?”), and tracks progress in real-time. If a task can be automated—like ordering the cake through an online bakery or syncing a meeting invite to your team’s calendars—Florque will propose the tool, ask for your go-ahead, and execute it seamlessly upon approval. It’s like having a proactive planner that not only organizes but also does the heavy lifting, reducing stress and ensuring everything runs smoothly, whether it’s a birthday bash or a business project.
